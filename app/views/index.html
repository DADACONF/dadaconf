We believe that anyone has the ability to make art.

Most art doesn’t take special talent to make. It just takes a bit of perseverance and maybe a couple friendly faces helping you figure things out along the way. At Dadaconf, we want people to learn something new or teach other folks the things they know.

Whether you are an artist, musician, engineer, or anything else, you can make something fun, strange, and absurd at Dadaconf.

Dadaconf 0.1

The goal of Dadaconf is to brings lots of people together to create things at the intersection of art, tech, and music.

Dadaconf is a very freeform event: the main events are the Friday night opening ceremony and the Saturday night exhibition and party. Saturday evening people will have an opportunity to exhibit and demonstrate their creative projects. We'll also have performances and end the event with a big party.

The rest of the weekend is dedicated to giving people time to work on creative projects, attend workshops, and learn new things. There will be opportunities to meet others and collaborate. We'll also be setting up a way for you to get in touch with other attendees prior to the event in case you want to organize beforehand.

Dadaconf attendees will be offering various workshops, lessons, and events to learn and explore cool creative tech.see past workshops

Big Dada

Dadaconf celebrates [Big Dada], an art movement playing on two concepts: 1) the original Dada of the 1920s and 2) the buzzwordy and absurd tech industry that is an increasing presence in everyone's life.

Big Dada is about creating experiences that explore technology and creativity. It's about rejecting tech's obsession with consumption and making fast money. It’s about focusing on creating, doing, and learning.
