Getting to NIMBY

Parking

NIMBY is located at 8410 Amelia St in Oakland. There is limited parking available anywhere on Amelia st. We recommend carpooling or taking BART however, since there is limited parking by the venue.

Public Transportation

NIMBY is accessible by a 20 minute walk from the Coliseum BART Station. It's definitely a safe walk over during the day. At night, you may want to consider carpooling or catching Lyft/Uber to the BART station.
