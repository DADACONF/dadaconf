Open Source

We request that all work created at Dadaconf be released under open source to whatever extent possible.

Why? We’ve all learned so much from free materials — free software, instructions, tutorials, and more — and we’d like to add to that. Technology has the greatest potential when it’s available to everyone. We believe that information wants to be free.

We’re big believers in open source. Whether it’s software foundational to our daily lives that’s freely available to all, watching groups of disparate people collaborate to create something useful, or picking something apart to learn about how it works, open source is the great equalizer in technology.

You can check out the source for this website, as well as projects from previous Dadaconfs and their documentation under our Github page. Future projects created at Dadaconf will also be released on our Github.
